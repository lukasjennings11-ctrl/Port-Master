# CoolMath Games — submission pitch

CoolMath Games licenses/features family-friendly browser games. Submit via their site's
**"Submit a Game"** form (coolmathgames.com → footer → *Submit Your Game*, or the partner/business
contact). Port Boss is a strong fit: all-ages, no violence, no gambling (use the **gambling-free
build** — the Poki/bare build with the wager event removed), instant browser play, works on mobile.

Keep it short and professional. Below is a ready-to-send version.

---

**Subject:**
```
Game submission: Port Boss — free HTML5 idle tycoon (all-ages, mobile + desktop)
```

**Body:**
```
Hi CoolMath Games team,

I'd like to submit my game Port Boss for consideration.

Port Boss is a free, browser-based idle/tycoon game: you found a harbour on a wild coast and grow it from a small fishing village all the way to a futuristic megaport — building, upgrading, running trade routes, and sending expeditions across eight ages. It's a relaxed, build-at-your-own-pace game with a friendly 3D papercraft look.

Why it fits CoolMath:
- All-ages and school-safe: no violence, no blood, no bad language, and no gambling of any kind (this build has no wager mechanics at all).
- Instant HTML5 — no install, no account, no login wall. Plays immediately in the browser.
- Works on both mobile and desktop (responsive, touch + mouse).
- Lightweight: loads in seconds, ~1 MB initial.
- Lots of depth for return visits: production chains, a fleet you upgrade, trade routes, expeditions, and prestige.

You can play it right now here: https://ljennings11.itch.io/port-boss

It's non-exclusive and I can provide a build packaged to your requirements (including your SDK/ad wrapper if you use one). Happy to send cover art, a gameplay video, or any format you need.

Thanks for taking a look,
Lukas Jennings
lukasjennings11@gmail.com
```

### Notes for you
- Attach or link the **landscape cover** (`submit/crazygames/cover-landscape-1920x1080.png`) and the
