# Port Boss — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 16 July 2026_

Port Boss ("the game", "we") is a single-player idle/tycoon game. This policy explains what data
the game does and does not handle. In short: **Port Boss collects no personal information and has no
accounts, no tracking, and no third-party analytics.**

## What we collect
**Nothing personal.** The game does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any account.
There is no sign-up and no login.

## Data stored on your device
Your game progress (your harbours, buildings, money, upgrades, settings and achievements) is saved
**only on your own device**, using local storage on the web version and the app's local storage on
the mobile app. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is not
readable by us. Deleting the app (or clearing your browser's site data) removes it.

## Analytics and tracking
The game uses **no third-party analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking cookies or
SDKs**. The mobile app contains **no advertising**.

## Third parties
- The **mobile app** (iOS App Store / Google Play) sends no data to us or to any third party.
- The **web version** is hosted on standard web hosting and, when played through a game portal
  (e.g. CrazyGames, Poki), that portal's own privacy policy governs anything the portal does around
  the game frame; Port Boss itself still stores progress only on your device.

## Children
Port Boss is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, we do not knowingly collect
information from children (or anyone).

## Changes
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new "Last updated" date.
